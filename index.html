Introducing WELFIE

Welfie is inexpensive smartphone based personal blood pressure measuring device for you and your family. It is designed to solve the hassle of taking BP and sharing readings with your doctor.

What we are trying to solve

These are the problems that we have been trying to solve. Please let us know if you can connect these problems

Measurement Accuracy

Available consumer BP machines are not that accurate, hence doctors don't rely on the reading when shared

Keeping History

BP histroy can reveal important health issues but most people maintain history in some log book which is not useful.

Sharing Records

Sharing log books with doctors is not a efficient way of sharing data for analysis

REMINDERS

Some patients forget to take their daily BP measurement and make record of it.

Welfie Software

Features of Application

Our android app uses machine learning and good design to improve the overall experience

  • Accurate BP - Using ML for accurate readings
  • Health Prediction - Using ML for predicting any health issue
  • History - Record history for multiple users
  • Share - Share history with just one click
  • Reminders - Get daily reminders for taking BP readings

Welfie Hardware

Features of hardware

Our hardware uses minimal electronics to keep the price and size small

  • Affordable price
  • Easy setup - Plug it in your smart phone & launch app
