EASY SETUP

Welfie doesn't require pairing or authentication to setup. It is just as easy as plugging your earphone in your smartphone. We are using 3.5mm audio jack for sending data because it is common in almost all smartphone.

MEASUREMENT ACCURACY

White coat syndrome, cuff size or unwanted body movement can cause inaccuracy in BP measurements. Welfie uses machine learning to treat these problems carefully to improve the accuracy of measurements.

HEALTH PREDICTION

Patients are not proficient to make useful insight from their BP reading history but Welfie can and it will tell you when you should visit doctor.

KEEPING HISTORY

Patients diagnosed with chronic ailments are recommended to maintain their BP records for future analysis but physical recordings make it difficult. Also, tracking the history of more than one person can be challenging.
Welfie provides a better solution to maintain records.

SHARING HISTORY

It is crucial for some patient to regularly share their BP records with doctor but sharing physical records are difficult.
Welfie provides a platform to record and share measurements with doctor anytime.

REMINDERS

Patients often forget to take their daily medication and BP measurements , which may lead to some serious health issues.
